Hajj Umrah guidance

Introduction

The purpose of the hajj/umrah app is to improve the timeliness and accuracy of hajj/umrah operations and to provide services to all the pilgrims who go every year to perform hajj/ umrah.
So that they won’t face any difficulty. The project deliverables shall include hajj/umrah system design, all coding, testing, implementation of an integrated system with a user’s guide.
High level risks for this project include aunthentication as this is a very sensitive topic and ensuring implementation is completed without impacting ongoing hajj/umrah operations.
What? Hajj Umrah Guidance Application.
When? June,2018- June,2019
Why? So that pilgrims do not face any difficulty due to unawareness.
Where? Univesity of Gujrat as final year project.
Who? For pilgrims made by group members.
How? Android Application.

Functionalities

  • Login
  • Tips before leaving for hajj/ Umrah.
  • Hajj Guidance Lectures
  • Umrah Guidance lectures
  • Important dua
  • Notifications alerts
  • Quran
  • Suggestions
  • Counter
  • Prayer timing
  • Zakat calculator
